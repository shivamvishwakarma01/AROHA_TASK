About Aasha

Empowering underprivileged children through creative expression

Our Story

Conducted under Project Anand, Aasha is a series of events and activities that provide children from underprivileged communities with opportunities to explore and showcase their talents. Founded in 2020, we've impacted over 500 children across Delhi NCR.

Our Mission

The mission of Aasha is to nurture the hidden talents of these children and offer them a stage to confidently present their abilities. We aim to empower them to realize their potential and pursue their dreams through artistic expression and skill development.
